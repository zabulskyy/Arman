General info about the program & requirements

  • Offers 44 different courses
  • 4 years of studying + practice in one of the leading companies of the country
  • Opportunity to study abroad for one semester
  • Scholarship for top 10 students of the year
  • To apply for our program you have to pass IHSGE(Internatoinal High School Graduation Exam) in mathematics, English, and physics, with grade more than 65%. Otherwise your application wont be considered. Other details about required documentation will be sent on your email after the interview.
